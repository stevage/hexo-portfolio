Hipster Map of Melbourne

A snarky map of cafes, bars, shopping and other cultural oddities.
Tech: Mapbox-GL-JS

The joke that went too far, this silly idea was cooked up by good friend Maia Sauren, who thought it would be a good community-building exercise for Open Knowledge Melbourne. She led the team of snark content creators, someone else did the original design and I put it together. About

I'm Steve Bennett, a freelance web developer based in Melbourne, Australia. I build websites and mapping infrastructure, specialising in Mapbox, vector tiles, NodeJS and VueJS.
My past experience includes contributing to Wikipedia and OpenStreetMap, working in the government and academic sectors on the general theme of data sharing and management.
